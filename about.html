Ignatius Global School is a great school. It has 14 classes for just 10th grade. It's facilities are: a pool, a library, lab computers, chemistry labs, a place to play soccer basketball, badminton, table tennis and all other stuff. The 10th grade are divided into two, in which the first 10 classes are for those who wanted in on IPA(Ilmu Pengetahuan Alam), while the rest 4 classes are for IPS(Ilmu Pengetahuan Sosial).
